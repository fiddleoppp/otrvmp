Delivery Info and Charges

We have 2 delivery options, all trackable.

Option 1: Delivery

  • Two Options:

    • Home Delivery: 9am - 10pm on Weekdays (Mon-Fri) and Saturdays, no delivery on Public Holidays
    • Office Delivery: 9am - 10pm on Weekdays (Mon-Fri), no delivery on Saturdays and Public Holidays
  • $4.90 per order
  • Local delivery with tracking number, our courier is Ninja Van
  • Usually delivered between 5-8 working days* for over 10 million books labeled as Available

Option 2: Self-Collection at Ninja Points

  • October 2021: This option is temporarily not available.
  • Over 1000 self-collect locations islandwide
  • Conveniently-located at shopping malls, bus and MRT stations
  • $2.49 per order
  • Usually ready for collection between 5-8 working days* for over 10 million books labeled as Available
  • More information on self-collect locations, please click here

Take note:

  • Fee is on per order basis, not per book
  • For example: you buy 5 books and choose Office Delivery, delivery fee is $4.90
  • *Working days are Mondays through Fridays excluding Public Holidays
  • Saturdays, Sundays and Public Holidays are not counted as working days
  • Public Holidays in 2021:
    http://www.mom.gov.sg/employment-practices/public-holidays
  • Delivery is not available to these areas:

URGENT DELIVERY - Can I get books earlier than 5-8 working days?

  • We regret that this is strictly not possible
  • Books labeled "Available" are usually fulfilled (even for self-collection) within 8 working days, barring unforeseen circumstances
  • We cannot guarantee that we can fulfill within the most optimistic estimate of 5 working days
  • To be on the safe side, you would need to assume fulfillment time of 8 working days (barring unforeseen circumstances)
  • If you are getting books for someone's birthday or other important events, please ensure to order the books 8 working days before
